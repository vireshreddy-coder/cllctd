How It Works

From raw data
to licensed dataset

Four steps from contributor to enterprise buyer — with cllctd handling everything in between.

01
🤝
Vendor onboarding
Hardware partners, voice networks, and individual contributors register and connect their data supply — devices, speaker pools, archives, or camera rolls.
02
⚡
Ingestion & consent
Data flows into our pipeline with consent captured at source. Quality scored, deduplicated, formatted. Every contributor KYC-verified for payout eligibility.
03
🏷️
Annotation & packaging
Human-in-the-loop annotation enriches raw data with labels, transcripts, bounding boxes, action tags. Packaged to buyer-specified schemas and formats.
04
🚀
Licensed to buyers
Enterprise buyers access via catalogue, API, or custom commission. Licensing revenue split with vendors and contributors — every sale, automatically.
