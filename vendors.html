Common Questions

What vendors
ask us.

Do I need a registered company to apply?

No — individuals and unregistered networks can apply. We recommend having a registered entity before the first deal closes as enterprise buyers require invoices. We can help.

What if I don't have consent documentation yet?

Apply anyway. "Not yet established" is a valid answer. Our legal team provides consent framework templates and guidance to get you compliant.

Can I list on cllctd and sell directly to buyers too?

Yes. Non-exclusive listings allow you to license through multiple channels. Only disclose prior licensing when a buyer requests exclusivity.

How does cllctd handle my data during QA?

Sample datasets are reviewed in a secure environment. Raw data is never shared with buyers until a licensing agreement is signed. You retain ownership throughout.

What languages and regions do you prioritise?

Active demand is highest for Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Marathi, Kannada, and Gujarati. Egocentric worker video is in extremely high demand regardless of language.

When and how do I get paid?

Payment within 14 days of deal closing. Methods: bank transfer (SWIFT/NEFT), UPI, and USDC. Currency negotiated at onboarding.
