How It Works

Four steps to
getting paid.

📲

Join & Verify

Sign up with your phone number. Quick identity verification. Takes 3 minutes. No documents needed to start.

📋

Pick a Task

Browse available tasks in the app. Filter by type, language, and pay rate. New tasks appear every day based on active buyer demand.

✅

Submit & Get Approved

Complete the task on your phone. Our QA system reviews when approved. Approval rate for first-time contributors is 85%+.

💸

Get Paid

Earnings paid on approval. Withdraw via UPI, bank transfer, or convert to USDC. Minimum payout: ₹100.
